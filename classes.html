Convenient learning

De La Salle Kirenge Brothers School is a school that follows good traditions based on Christian values. Students are taught to pray, respect others, and work hard in their studies. The school also teaches Rwandan culture through traditional dances, songs, and language. Learners live like a family, helping each other and learning with discipline and love. These traditions help students grow with strong morals and a bright future.

school Theater

ProFuturo is a digital education project that helps students learn using tablets and computers. It started in Rwanda in 2019 and supports many primary schools by giving learning materials, training teachers, and using technology in class. While De La Salle Kirenge is not officially listed, it may be part of the program through its Lasallian network. The project helps improve education by making learning easier and more fun for students.
