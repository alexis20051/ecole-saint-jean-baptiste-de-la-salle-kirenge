about Ecole Saint jean Baptiste De La salle Kirenge

Kirenge, We have been teaching Students Since 2006

De La Salle Kirenge School is a well-established educational institution located in Rwanda. The school offers quality education across three major levels:

Nursery Section:

This section provides a strong foundation for young children, focusing on early childhood development through fun, interactive learning and care.

Primary Section:

The primary level offers a well-rounded curriculum that builds literacy, numeracy, critical thinking, and moral values, preparing students for further education.

O'Level (Lower Secondary):

At the O’Level, students receive more specialized instruction in core subjects, helping them transition into advanced secondary studies or vocational training.
