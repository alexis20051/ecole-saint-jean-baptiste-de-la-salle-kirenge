about our School

Just study, We have been teaching since 2006


De La Salle Kirenge is located in Rulindo district kisaro sector it was established in 2006 by the Brothers of Chirstian Schools and it is a day school and bording school.

De La Salle Kirenge School is a well-established educational institution located in Rwanda.
The school offers quality education across three major levels.
De La Salle Kirenge is a Christian school guided by strong values and principles. It is a brothers’ school, providing a supportive and disciplined environment for students. The O’Level section operates as a boarding school, allowing learners to focus on their studies while living on campus.

Nursery Section:

This section provides a strong foundation for young children, focusing on early childhood development through fun, interactive learning and care.

Primary Section:

The primary level offers a well-rounded curriculum that builds literacy, numeracy, critical thinking, and moral values, preparing students for further education.

O'Level:

At the O’Level, students receive more specialized instruction in core subjects, helping them transition into advanced secondary studies or vocational training.
